Biography

I’m a graduate student at the Penn State University advised by Prof. C. Lee Giles and Prof. Dan Kifer and most recently I have worked with the Camera team, Qualcomm San-Diego as a computer vision intern for summer 2020. My research interests are visual perception (object recognition & localization, segmentation), 3D Vision and Affective Computing/Emotion Modelling. I graduated from Delhi College of Engineering, India in 2019 with a bachelor’s degree in ECE and was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Rajesh Rohilla. In the summer of 2018, I interned with the computer vision team at University of Manitoba, mentored by Prof. Yang Wang. Earlier, I have also visited Prof. Vinay Namboodiri at IIT-Kanpur and Prof. Madasu Hanmandlu at IIT-Delhi as a research intern.
